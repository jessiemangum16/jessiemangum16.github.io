Délice French Bakery & Cafe

In November of 2018, Jean-Jacques Grossi and Akbar Matinkhah open Délice French Bakery and Café. The mission was to create pastries and cakes that brought a taste of France to the Salt Lake Valley.

Chef Jean-Jacques Grossi started cooking and baking at a young age in the South of France, moving to the Greater Salt Lake area in 1977. He’s been in the food industry ever since, and with over 45 years of experience, the most delightful recipes are brought to life. With his personal touch and classic French recipes, we are proud to offer a variety of hand-crafted sweet bites to the Salt Lake City community. Our chef’s favorite treat? The Feuillantine cake.

Purpose

The main purpose of Salt Lake City Chamber of commerce is to promotion local businesses and bring businesses together to grow and succeed to the best of their abilities. It is also be a place for customers to locate locally owned companies. It is important to purchase from smaller locally owned companies to endure success.
